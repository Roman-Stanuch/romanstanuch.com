After previous meddlings in game development with Unreal and Unity, and computer graphics with OpenGL, my current projects center around modern web dev and cybersecurity.

Studies

I will be starting college this upcoming fall, so most of my knowledge up until this point has been self taught. Most of my learning has come from building projects and looking at documentation, but some sources I found especially helpful were Stephen Ulibarri's courses for learning Unreal and Harvard's CS50w for learning the basics of web development. Some courses are good for starting out, but I think the fastest way to learn is just to go out and build something, struggle, fail, make mistakes, but come out a better programmer in the end.
